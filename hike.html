New Year's Day Hike

1st January 2021 at 08:00 until 16:30

Glendalough, Co. Wicklow

View Map

10 Tickets Remaining

Details

This would be a fairly easy route but we've made it a bit more challenging: after about 2km, we plan to leave the route and ascend the prominent boulder field, heading up towards the two fangs of rock near the top.

This is not especially difficult but it can be disconcerting to feel some of the large boulders pivot when you put your weight on them! Hands-on scrambling required.

We've got a couple of other deviations from the normal route in mind too.

Return will be either on the Sugarloaf Way or on a different track that goes around the other side of the mountain, coming out at the GAA grounds.

Current government regulations specify a strict limit of 15 people for outdoor events.
